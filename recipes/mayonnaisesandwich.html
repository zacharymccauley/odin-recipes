Mayonnaise Sandwich

Description

The classic mayonnaise sandwich is a common staple among poverty meals. It is a simple, modest dish consisting of whatever white bread you may have lying around and your choice of mayonnaise. This author recommends Duke's Brand Mayonnaise for added texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Any White Bread
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Spread a healthy amount of mayonnaise across both slices of bread
  2. Press slices of brad together
  3. Cut sandwich into at least two portions to feign quantity
  4. ENJOY (:
