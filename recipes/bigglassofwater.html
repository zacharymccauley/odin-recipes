Big Ol Glass of Water

Description

Occasionally you need to just fill your belly and stop the ol rumbly tumblies. A big cold glass of water will stop those urges for a while and at least make you feel less like crap!

Ingredients

  • A, hopefully, clean glass
  • Ice
  • Water that wont give you dysentary

Steps

  1. Place desired amount of ice in glass
  2. Fill your glass full of water
  3. Drink drink drink!!
